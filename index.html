Extracted text (edit before converting)

Anything with labels like “Ancestry: …”, “STR 13”, “AC 14” and section headers (Talents / Gear / Spells). Best-effort — you’ll edit the result.

What carries across exactly (JSON): name, ancestry, class, level, title, alignment, background, deity, AC, HP, all six stats + computed mods, XP, gold, gear-slot table, spells, languages, ancestry talent, and every rolled talent with its description.
What it can’t know: a class’s fixed features (e.g. a third-party Seawolf’s Shield Wall) aren’t in the export — add those in the Talents block. Magic weapon bonuses and portrait filename are left as editable stubs. Roleplay/behavior text is a blank scaffold in your format.
Single file. Save it to your GitHub Pages repo and add to your Home Screen, same as HENCHMAN. No data leaves your device on the JSON/PDF/text paths.
